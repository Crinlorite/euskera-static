This lesson brings together everything from topic 10. Here you practise something concrete and useful: **describing three of your neighbours** with simple but connected sentences. It's the A1 acid test — and it's exactly what gets worked on in the first classroom introductions.

## Standard structure (recommended order)

For each neighbour, always follow these pieces. If you have them, the description builds itself:

1. **Introduction** — *Hau nire bizilaguna da. Bera X-koa da.* (where they're from)
2. **Age / marital status** — *X urte ditu. Ezkonduta / Bakarrik dago.*
3. **Profession** — *Bera Y da.* or *Y-an lan egiten du.*
4. **Character** — *Oso jatorra da.* or *Lasaia / Berritsua da.*
5. **A personal detail** — something you observe: when you see them, what they do.

> You don't need to say all five things. With three, you already have a description.

## Vocabulary for a neighbour's character

| Euskera | English |
|---|---|
| *jatorra* | nice, warm, approachable |
| *atsegina* | pleasant |
| *alaia* | cheerful |
| *lasaia* | calm/quiet |
| *isila* | quiet, reserved |
| *berritsua* | chatty |
| *zaratatsua* | noisy |
| *zakarra* | rude / brusque |
| *jakintsua* | wise |

## Model text

> *Nire atarian hiru bizilagun nagusi ditugu — bakoitza solairu batean.*
>
> ***Lehenengoa Marina da.*** *Bera 70 urteko jubilatua da, lehenengo solairuan bizi da. Bakarrik bizi da, baina txakur batekin. Oso atsegina eta berritsua da: igogailuan beti hitz egiten du.*
>
> ***Bigarrena Egoitz da.*** *Bera kazetaria da eta egunkari batean lan egiten du. Bere bikotekidearekin bizi da bigarren solairuan. Lasaia da, ez dugu ia entzuten.*
>
> ***Hirugarrena Hodei eta Maialen dira.*** *Hauek nire ikaskideak ere badira: euskaltegira elkarrekin joaten gara. Hirugarren solairuan bizi dira eta ikasleak dira. Alaiak eta zaratatsuak dira: asteburuetan musika ozen entzuten dute.*

## Useful connecting phrases

If you want to link your descriptions better, these simple connectors will help you:

| Euskera | English |
|---|---|
| *eta* | and |
| *baina* | but |
| *baita ere* | also |
| *gainera* | besides, moreover |
| *adibidez* | for example |
| *batzuetan* | sometimes |

> E.g.: *Bera lasaia da, **baina** asteburuetan zaratatsua bihurtzen da. **Adibidez**, ostiraletan musika entzuten du gauean.*

> **Final tip:** your first descriptions of neighbours won't be perfect. What matters is that the listener understands **who they are**, **what they do** and **how you see them**. With that foundation, the grammar corrections come naturally with practice.
