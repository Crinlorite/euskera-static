There are two equally correct ways to ask someone's name:

- **Zein da zure izena?** — "What is your name?"
- **Zure izena zein da?** — the word order can vary; both are natural.

## Structure

- **Zein** = which / what (in questions involving a choice)
- **izena** = the name (the final `-a` is the article "the")
- **zure** = your / yours
- **da** = is

## How to answer

You have two options, equally valid:

1. **Nire izena Egoitz da.** — "My name is Egoitz."
2. **Egoitz naiz.** — "I am Egoitz." (the pattern from the previous lesson)

> The basic possessives are *nire* (my), *zure* (your), *bere* (his/her). You'll see them many more times later on — start getting familiar with them now.
