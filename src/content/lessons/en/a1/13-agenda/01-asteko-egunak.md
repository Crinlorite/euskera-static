Before talking about plans and schedules, let's review the days of the week and learn to associate them with everyday activities.

## The seven days

| Basque | English | Hint |
|---|---|---|
| *astelehena* | Monday | aste + lehen = "first day of the week" |
| *asteartea* | Tuesday | aste + arte = "midweek" |
| *asteazkena* | Wednesday | aste + azken = "last day of the working week" (hist.) |
| *osteguna* | Thursday | egun = day |
| *ostirala* | Friday | shares the root *ortzi/osti-* (sky) with *osteguna* |
| *larunbata* | Saturday | larun + bata |
| *igandea* | Sunday | historical feast day |

> **Memory hint:** the word **aste** (week) appears at the beginning of the weekdays. *Astelehen-, astear-, asteazken-* almost spell out aloud the beginning, middle and end of the week.

## Saying "on Monday" — the suffix `-(e)an`

To date an event, you add `-(e)an` to the day:

| Day | "On + day" |
|---|---|
| astelehena | astelehen**ean** |
| asteartea | astearte**an** |
| asteazkena | asteazken**ean** |
| osteguna | ostegun**ean** |
| ostirala | ostirale**an** |
| larunbata | larunbat**ean** |
| igandea | igande**an** |

- *Astelehenean lan egiten dut.* — I work on Monday.
- *Larunbatean afaltzera joango gara.* — On Saturday we will go out for dinner.

## Saying "every X" — the suffix `-ero`

For regular frequency:

- *astelehenero* — every Monday
- *larunbatero* — every Saturday
- *egunero* — every day
- *astero* — every week
- *astebururo* — every weekend

## Day + activity: examples

| Basque | English |
|---|---|
| *Astelehenean kiroldegira noa.* | On Monday I go to the gym. |
| *Asteartean euskara klasea dut.* | On Tuesday I have a Basque class. |
| *Ostiralean parranda egiten dugu.* | On Friday we go out partying. |
| *Larunbatean familiaren etxera joango naiz.* | On Saturday I will go to the family's house. |
| *Igandean lasai gelditzen naiz etxean.* | On Sunday I stay home and relax. |

## Asteburua — the weekend

`Asteburua` (weekend) is a very useful word:

- *Asteburuan zer egingo duzu?* — What are you going to do on the weekend?
- *Asteburu honetan mendira joango naiz.* — This weekend I will go to the mountains.
- *Asteburu pasa egitea gustatzen zait.* — I like to take weekend getaways.

> **Cultural note:** alongside *igandea* you will often see *jaieguna* (holiday/feast day): the phrase *igandeak eta jaiegunak* is typical on bus timetables, shops and town-hall notices.
