You already have all the pieces: types of restaurant, the menu, ordering, paying. Let's put them together in a dinner with friends: arranging the date, ordering and wrapping up the evening.

## 1. Arranging to have dinner

> — *Aizue, ostiralean afaltzera joango gara?* — Hey, shall we go out to dinner on Friday?
>
> — *Bai, ideia ona!* — Yes, good idea!
>
> — *Nik ezin dut ostiralean. Larunbatean ondo?* — I can't on Friday. Is Saturday okay?
>
> — *Ados. Zer ordutan geratuko gara?* — All right. What time shall we meet?
>
> — *Bederatzi eta erdietan, taberna ondoan?* — At half past nine, next to the bar?
>
> — *Ederki!* — Perfect!

Phrases for excusing yourself when you can't:

- **Ezin dut, beste plan bat dut.** — I can't, I have another plan.
- **Ezin dut, bihar lan egin behar dut.** — I can't, tomorrow I have to work.
- **Beste egun batean izan beharko du.** — It will have to be another day.

## 2. Making the reservation

> — *Egun on! "Kutixi" jatetxea al da?*
>
> — *Bai, esan.*
>
> — *Erreserba egin nahi dut, larunbat gauerako.*
>
> — *Zenbat lagunentzat?*
>
> — *Lau lagun izango gara.*
>
> — *Zer ordutan?*
>
> — *Bederatzi eta erdietan.*
>
> — *Noren izenean?*
>
> — *Mikelen izenean.*
>
> — *Oso ondo. Larunbat arte!*

## 3. At the table

Greetings when sitting down:

- **On egin!** — Enjoy your meal!
- **Topa!** — Cheers! / To your health! (when toasting)

Ordering, now with all the pieces:

> — *Hasteko, niretzat entsalada mistoa, mesedez.*
>
> — *Eta niretzat arrain-zopa.*
>
> — *Bigarrenerako, niretzat legatza saltsan.*
>
> — *Niri, txuleta. Eta edateko, ardo gorria botila bat, mesedez.*

## 4. Commenting on the food

| Euskera | English |
|---|---|
| *Oso goxoa zegoen!* | It was very good! |
| *Hau betekada!* | What a feast! |
| *Beste botila bat ardo, mesedez.* | Another bottle of wine, please. |
| *Postrerako, izozkia hartuko dut.* | For dessert, I'm going to have ice cream. |
| *Nik ez dut postrerik nahi.* | I don't want dessert. |

## 5. Wrapping up the evening

> — *Aizu, kobratuko al diguzu?*
>
> — *Bai, oraintxe. Berrogeita hamabi euro eta hogeita bost zentimo.*
>
> — *Txartelaz ordain dezakegu?*
>
> — *Bai, noski.*
>
> — *Eskerrik asko! Oso ondo zegoen.*
>
> — *Zuri! Hurrengora arte!*

## Visual summary

```
1. Arrange:     Etorri nahi duzu afaltzera?  →  Bai, ados!
2. Reservation: Erreserba egin nahi dut...   →  Noren izenean?
3. Order:       Hasteko... Bigarrenerako...  →  Postrerako...
4. Enjoy:       On egin! / Topa! / Goxoa zegoen!
5. Pay:         Kontua, mesedez. / Txartelaz ordain dezaket?
```

> **Cultural note:** the phrase *On egin!* is also used when you walk past people who are eating in a public place (a terrace, a fruit shop, anywhere). It's a very typical polite gesture worth hearing and returning: the natural reply is *Eskerrik asko!*.
